I am a graduate student in Applied Mathematics at West Virginia University. I have a bachelors in Aerospace Engineering, and a Masters in Mechanical Engineering from West Virginia University.

I recently completed Google Summer of Code 2016 at the MIT Center for Mobile Learning. Through this Open Source project, I integrated RaspberryPi to support IoT (Internet of Things) on the MIT App Inventor platform. I am continuting contributions to the project, to develop a DIY low cost Weather Station Kit.

In the recent past I worked as the android programming instructor at the WVU LaunchLab. I was also a visiting graduate research student at the MIT's Laboratory for Autonomous Marine Sensing Systems in Cambridge MA working on underwater autonomous vehicles.
